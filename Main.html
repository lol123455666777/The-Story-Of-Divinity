What is The Story Of Divinity?

A Brief

The Story Of Divinity is animated series describing the immense qualities of Pramukh Swami Maharaj. From Selfless Service to Unbreakable Tolerance, This pays tribute to his Compassionate service to Humanity and His Life Motto "In The Joy Of Lies Our Own".The Story of Divinity is 7 episode series depicting his different qualities and will be a Journey Of A Lifetime about a Young boy.....

Character Insite

Who Are The Characters?

Arjun

An average and Non-Satsangi person who is amazed by Pramukh Swami Maharaj divine qualities which made him desire and grasp every little detail about Pramukh Swami Maharaj.By the end of the series Arjun turns into a Satsangi. He and Kirtan are friends but neither know the true nature of Pramukh Swami Maharaj.

Kirtan

Your standard satsangi who doesn’t understand Pramukh Swami Maharaj’s true greatness but is working towards it and the stories that he hears gives him the final push to becoming a true and ideal balak.

Divya

An eager friend and fellow satsangi who wishes to preach Satsang to his friends and the entire world.To do this he presents a assembly to his school which leads him to meet Arjun who he teaches about Pramukh Swami Maharaj’s tolerance.

Harit

A smart and well known friend of Arjun and Kirtan who informs them about BAPS and Neasden Temple. He also acknowledges them on how Pramukh Swami Maharaj changes their lives as well not just them but countless more.
